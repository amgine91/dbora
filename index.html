Welcome to Discover Boracay Travel Resort

Tourism is one of the fastest growing Industries in the Philippines and Boracay Island had recently won the title “Best Tropical Island” around the world. Department of Tourism reported that the island welcomes almost 400,000 visitors yearly from all over the world. Kalibo International Airport started accepting direct flights from Korea, Taiwan and China. It was recently announced by the Governor that Singapore flights will be soon available. Most of the foreign tourists are Koreans, Chinese, US, Germany, UK, and Taiwanese. Since then, Discover Boracay Travel and Tours was formed to provide travel packages to this enormous number of tourists that visited Boracay Island. The current trend towards the growing number of tourist that enters the Island of Boracay presents a big opportunity for Discover Boracay Travel and Tours to penetrate the market sector in the service industry. We would like to take advantage of the moderate growth of the competition in the province travel at the moment, with competent and dedicated personnel, effective management and well trained marketing to take care of our valued clientele.
